Cancer Detection Project:
Levraging CNN Models in Detecting Lung Cancer in histopathological images

In this project, I aim to develop a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model to detect lung cancer in histopathological images. The model classifies images into three categories: 'lung_aca' (lung adenocarcinoma), 'lung_n' (normal), and 'lung_scc' (squamous cell carcinoma). Using TensorFlow and Keras, I trained the model over 40 epochs, with accuracy as the primary evaluation metric. Throughout the process, I leveraged Matplotlib for visualizing the model's performance. After training, the model achieved a train loss of 0.0154 and a train accuracy of 99.77%. The validation loss was 0.0423, with a validation accuracy of 99.06%. On the test set, the model obtained a test loss of 0.0376 and a test accuracy of 98.75%.
