Cancer Detection Project:
Leveraging CNN Models in Detecting Lung Cancer in Histopathological Images

A CNN model was developed to detect lung cancer from histopathological images. Images were classified into lung adenocarcinoma, normal, and squamous cell carcinoma. The model achieved 99.77% training accuracy and 98.75% test accuracy. Confusion matrices and visualizations were used to evaluate performance.
